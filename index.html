About Us

Chucks Electric & Sons was founded in 1986 with a simple philosophy: do honest work at fair prices, and treat every customer's home like your own. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted family business now in its third generation.

We've wired everything from cozy lake cabins to commercial buildings, installed generators that kept families warm during nor'easters, and helped countless homeowners modernize their electrical systems safely.
