About Our Family

Our Story

Chucks Electric & Sons was founded in 1986 by Chuck Sr., a master electrician with a simple philosophy: do honest work at fair prices, and treat every customer's home like your own. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted family business now in its third generation.

We've wired everything from cozy lake cabins to commercial buildings, installed generators that kept families warm during nor'easters, and helped countless homeowners modernize their electrical systems safely. Our commitment to quality workmanship and customer service has remained constant through nearly four decades of service.
